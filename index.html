Mobile Casual Games

All of them have been commissioned by private clients and are not available on the app stores

Catch the kiss Game

The core of the game is to catch kisses from the girl by tapping on them trying to avoid loudy raspberries that lower your score.
The girl consists of a single sprite moved by an animator from right to left and reverse.

The school of Dance Game

The core of the game is to understand the position of the dancer and swipe your finger in the right direction as fast as you can in the limit time.
The dancer is made up by a set of different frames animated by finger's input through Unity built-in animator.

Frame-based animation prototypes

The dungeon game monster

This is the creation of a floating monster in a top-down 2D dungeon game.

The casual game frog

This is the functioning of a set of frames that interacts with a custom touch input system.
